Screenshots

Get started in 30 seconds

$ git clone https://github.com/Greal-dev/car-carer.git

$ cd car-carer && cp .env.example .env

# Add your ANTHROPIC_API_KEY and OPENROUTER_API_KEY

$ docker compose up -d

# Open http://localhost:8200

Features

AI Document Extraction

Gemini Flash 2.0 reads PDFs and photos. Extracts dates, costs, parts, mileage.

Intelligent Chat

Claude Sonnet 4 with 13 tools. Ask anything about your maintenance history.

Fuel Tracking

L/100km consumption charts, cost per km, station history.

Tax and Insurance

Track renewal dates, get expiry alerts. Never miss a deadline.

Vehicle Sharing

Share with your mechanic or family. Owner/editor/viewer roles.

PWA Mobile

Installable on phone. Camera capture for invoices on the go.

Smart Reminders

Trigger on km OR date. Whichever comes first.

Batch Upload

Import 50+ documents at once with real-time SSE progress.

4 Languages

French, English, German, Spanish. Easy to add more.

Car Carer is open-source (MIT License). Built with FastAPI, Claude, and Gemini.
